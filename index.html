By: Liliana Aguilar

One of my interests is attending music concerts. I really love live music and think its a fun thing to with family and friends. Some of my favorite shows I've seen were even here at the Santa Barbara Bowl! Some of my favorite shows I've seen include Tyler, The Creator, Coldplay, and Clairo but there are so many more!
